# Military Sleep Method

22 May 2026 (Source: Gemini)

1.  **Relax your face:** Close your eyes. Relax every muscle—forehead, eyelids, jaw, and tongue.
2.  **Drop your shoulders:** Let them sink as low as possible, followed by your upper and lower arms, one side at a time.
3.  **Exhale:** Relax your chest.
4.  **Clear your legs:** Relax thighs, calves, and feet.
5.  **Clear your mind:** For 10 seconds, imagine a calm scene (e.g., lying in a canoe on a calm lake) or repeat the words **\"Don't think, don't think, don't think\"** in your head.
