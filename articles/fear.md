# Fear fear or not?

5 June 2026 (Source: Gemini Unhinged)

1. Do not fear fear.

2. Fear Fear.

Here is the brief explanation:

1. **"Do not fear fear"**: This is an encouragement to accept anxiety or nervousness rather than fighting it. It suggests that the *fear of being afraid* is often worse than the actual emotion itself.

2. **"Fear Fear"**: This is a paradoxical warning. It suggests that fear is the most dangerous thing of all because it paralyzes you, clouds your judgment, and prevents you from taking action. In this sense, fear is the only thing truly worth fearing.
