# Repress vs. Supress

25 June 2026 (Source: [ChatGPT Private](https://chatgpt.com/c/6a3d6b24-7608-83e8-bb7d-a38677f98192))

**Suppress** and **repress** are related but different:

* **Suppress** = consciously hold back or stop something.

  * You know it's there and deliberately control it.
  * Example: *He suppressed his anger during the meeting.*

* **Repress** = unconsciously push thoughts, feelings, or memories out of awareness.

  * It happens psychologically, often without realizing it.
  * Example: *She may have repressed memories from childhood.*

A simple way to remember:

* **Suppress = conscious control**
* **Repress = unconscious hiding**

Example:

* *I wanted to shout, but I suppressed the urge.* ✅
* *He repressed painful emotions for years without realizing it.* ✅
