# Learn figjam

- ctrl+0: to reset to 100% zoom level
- r: rectangle
- o: elipse
- Ctrl+p and type diamond to get diamond shape
- Ctrl+p and type diamond to get triangle shape
- e: to get emoji and retype e to switch to stamps
- m: market
- shift+m: highlighter
- w: washi tape
- shift+backspae: eraser
