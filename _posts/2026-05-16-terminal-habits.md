Most productivity gains in development don’t come from giant breakthroughs. They usually come from tiny habits repeated every day — better shell aliases, cleaner configs, and knowing a few commands deeply.

Lately I’ve been trying to keep my setup simpler instead of endlessly customizing everything. Fewer tools, fewer distractions, and more focus on actually building things feels surprisingly effective.
